Introduction

Folk Wine Collective.

Folk Wine Collective is a San Luis Obispo based wine and spirits shop focused on sourcing wine and other alcoholic beverages from artisan wholesalers. We focus on serving thoughtfully produced beverages and supporting local makers. We are both a shop and a tasting room, with big open windows and shelves full of unique wines and other beverages.

Target audience

The people that use this website are those who are customers of Folk Wine Collective or are future customers of the shop.

Their primary tasks and goals are to get information about Folk and people who are looking to see which wines are in stock and available at the store. They are looking for certain beverages that are available and getting more info on the products. They are looking for natural and artisan wines and want to support local businesses and intentional brands.
